I'm a DST-Inspire faculty at Physics department in IISc Bangalore. Prior to this, I worked at Courant Institute (NYU) with Prof. Aleks Donev. Previously, I was at Institute of Materials (Köln) with Prof. Thomas Voigtmann and Prof. Jürgen Horbach. At Uni-Konstanz, I collaborated with Prof. Matthias Fuchs. I obtained a Ph.D. (Theoretical Physics) from The Institute of Mathematical Sciences (Chennai) after working with Prof. Gautam Menon and Prof. Ronojoy Adhikari.

I work on various (i) field theoretic problems, as well as (ii) particle level computational problems arising in soft materials at equilibrium and non-equilibrium scenario, mostly in studying of fluctuation effects as well as response to external perturbation of isotropic and anisotropic materials.
