Topics

  1. Simple Harmonic Motion:
    • Differential equation of SHM and its solutions. Kinetic and Potential Energy, Total Energy and their time averages.
    • Damped oscillations.
    • Forced Oscillations with harmonic forces. Compound pendulum.
  2. Elasticity:
    • Hooke's law, elastic moduli, relation between elastic constants, Poisson's Ratio, Expression for Poisson's ratio in terms of elastic constants.
    • Twisting couple on a cylinder - Determination of Rigidity modulus by static torsion. Torsional pendulum.
    • Bending of beams, Cantilever.
    • Work done in stretching and work done in twisting a wire.
