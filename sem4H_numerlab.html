Topics

  • Solution of ODE/PDE::
    • Initial Value Problem (IVP): Modified-Euler and Runge-Kutta second order and fourth order methods.
    • Boundary Value Problems (BVP): Finite discrete method with fixed step sizes. Idea of stability. Application to simple physical problems.
      • Laplace equation ∇2φ=0 on a square grid with specified potential at the boundaries.
      • Wave equation in 1+1 dimension: ∂t2φ=λ∂x2φ Vibration of a string with ends fixed with given initial configurations: φ(x,0) and ∂t(x,0).
      • Heat equation in 1+1 dimension, ∂tφ=α∂x2φ with specified value of temperature at the boundaries with given initial temperature at the boundaries with given initial temperature profile.
  • Dirac-delta function::
    • Numerically handling improper integrals over infinite intervals.
    • Evaluate 1⁄√2πσ2∫e-(x-2)2⁄2σ2 (x+3) dx for x = 1, 0.1, 0.01 and show that it tends to 5.
    • ∫-∞∞ exp[-(ax2+bx+c)] dx = π⁄aexp(b2⁄4a+c)
  • Fourier series:
    • Program to sum ∑0∞ (0.2)n.
    • Evaluate the fourier coefficients of a given periodic function (square wave).
  • Frobenius method and special functions:
    • ∫-1+1 Pn(μ)Pm(μ)dμ = δnm.
    • Plot Pn(x), jν(x).
    • Show recursion relation.
  • Evaluation of trigonometric functions e.g. sinθ. Given Bessel's function at N points find its value at an intermediate point.
  • Complex analysis:
    • Integrate ∫0∞ sinx/x dx numerically and check with computer integration.
    • Root finding:
      • Compute the nth roots of unity for n=2,3,and 4.
      • Find the two square roots of −5 + 12i .
  • Integral transform: FT of e-x2.
  • Introduction to OCTAVE.
