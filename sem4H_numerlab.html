Topics

  • Solution of ODE/PDE::
    • (a) Initial value problem: Modified-Euler and Runge-Kutta second order and fourth order methods. (b) Boundary value problems: Finite difference method with fixed step size. Application to simple physical problems.
  • Dirac-delta function::
    • Evaluate for 1⁄√2πσ2∫e-(x-2)2⁄2σ2 (x+3) dx . x=1,0.1,0.01 and show that it tends to 5.
  • Fourier series:
    • Program to sum ∑0∞ (0.2)n.
    • Evaluate the fourier coefficients of a given periodic function (square wave).
  • Frobenius method and special functions:
    • (a) ∫-1+1 Pn(μ)Pm(μ)dμ = δnm. (b) Plot Pn(x), jν(x). (c) show recursion relation.
  • Evaluation of trigonometric functions e.g. sinθ. Given Bessel's function at N points find its value at an intermediate point.
  • Complex analysis:
    • (a) Integrate numerically and check with computer integration. (b) Root finding:
      • Compute the nth roots of unity for n=2,3,and 4.
      • Find the two square roots of −5 + 12i .
  • Integral transform: FT of e-x2.
  • Introduction to OCTAVE.
