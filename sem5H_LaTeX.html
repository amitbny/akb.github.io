Project

  • Prepare a consolidated document (topic will be disclosed before University examination) incorporating all the sequels mentioned in syllabus on any physics subject that you are aware of. New intuitive and creative addition of LaTeX command that enriches the document qualitatively, quantitatively, aesthetically is welcome and will yield into more rewarding marks. On the contrary, an ill-performed document will reduce your marks significantly.
