Present Courses

  • Physics Honours (Semester System UG)

    • Semester I

      • PHS-A-CC-1-2-TH (Fluid Motion)

        [This course discusses on the general properties of matter with a focus on "flow properties of viscous fluid". Topics include Couette, Poiseuille flow and hydrostatics].

      • PHS-A-CC-1-2-TH (Non-Inertial Systems)

        [This course discusses on Classical Mechanics with a focus on non-intertial systems. This includes Galilean invariance, Coriolis Force, Rotating frames etc].

      • PHS-A-CC-1-1-P [Mathematical Physics I (Practical)]

        [This course discusses on numerical mathematics in scripting language Python. It includes basics of coding and performing arithmatic operations, solving algebraic equations in different geometry, root-finding, sorting and {mean,median,mode} of a number system, matrix addition, substraction and multiplication, curve fitting etc].

    • Semester II

      • PHS-A-CC-2-4-TH (Diffraction and Holography)

        [This course discusses on basic principles of diffraction and holography in Physical Optics. Topics include Fresnel and Fraunhofer diffraction, Holograms etc].

  • Physics Honours (1+1+1 System UG)

    • Paper VI (Unit II): Solid State Physics I

      [This course discusses on condensed matter physics with a focus on Crystalline and Amorphous solids. This includes Crystal structure, lattice planes, X-ray diffraction, crystal bonding, Free electron theory and band theory of solids, conductors, semiconductors, insulators, metals].

    • Paper VIIIB: Computer Laboratory

      [This course discusses on numerical mathematics in high level language C. It includes basics of coding and performing arithmatic operations, solving algebraic equations in different geometry, root-finding, sorting and {mean,median,mode} of a number system, matrix addition and multiplication, finding prime numbers on a given range, Iterative solvers, Interpolation and Integration method].

  • Physics General (Semester System UG)

    • Semester I: PHS-G-CC-1-1-TH (Oscillations & Elasticity)

      [This course discusses on the physics of Oscillations & theory of elasticity. This includes Simple Harmonic Motion, Superposition of SHM, forced damped SHM, Hooke's law, Torsion of Cylinder, Bending of beam, Twisting a wire etc].

    • Semester II: PHS-G-CC-2-2-TH (Induction & Maxwell's Equation)

      [This course discusses on Faraday's law of EM induction & Maxwell's equations with EM wave propagation. This inludes Faraday/Lenz's discovery, Motional EMF, Self/Mutual inductance, Displacement Current, Poynting Vector, Polarization etc].

  • Physics General (1+1+1 System UG)

    • Paper IVA: Communications

      [This course discusses on the physics of Communications. It motivates the subject by propagation of EM wave, transmission of such waves through media, modulation and demodulation].

    • Paper IVB: Computer Laboratory

      [This courses teaches about numerical mathematics in high level language C. It includes basic coding and performing arithmatic operations, solving various algebraic equations on different geometry, finding roots, sorting, matrix addition and substraction].

Previous Courses

  • Physics General Paper IB: Waves and Oscillations [This course discusses on the physics of Waves and Vibrations. This includes Simple Harmonic Motion, Superposition of SHM, forced damped SHM and various resonances, Wave equation in different geometry, Doppler effect] (2017).
