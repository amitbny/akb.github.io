Present Courses

  • Physics Honours (Semester System UG)

    1. Semester I

      • PHS-A-CC-1-2-TH (Fluid Motion) [The course discusses on the general properties of matter with a focus on "flow properties of viscous fluid". Topics include Couette, Poiseuille flow and hydrostatics. This course also introduces non-intertial frames of reference starting from Galilean relativity to Coriolis Force, Rotating frames & Foucault Pendulum].

      • PHS-A-CC-1-1-P [Mathematical Physics I (Practical)] [This course introduces "Numerical Mathematics" in language Python. It includes basics of arithmatic operations, roots of quadratic equation, sorting, {mean,median,mode} of a number system, matrix operations & curve fitting, plotting with Gnuplot].

    2. Semester II

      • PHS-A-CC-2-4-TH (Diffraction) [This course discusses on the basic principles of diffraction in Physical Optics. Topics include Fresnel and Fraunhofer diffraction].

    3. Semester III

      • PHS-A-CC-3-6-TH (Thermodynamics) [This course discusses on non-equilibrium/nearly-equilibrium Thermodynamics. It initiates with various laws of Thermodynamics, potentials, low temperature physics and phase equilibria].

      • PHS-A-CC-3-5-P [Mathematical Physics II (Practical)] [This course elaborates on Numpy, Scipy and Matplotlib. It includes solving (coupled) linear equations of various order, constructing special functions, root-finding, interpolation, differentiation and integration, explicit time-steppers and 3D graph plotting].

    4. Semester IV

      • PHS-A-CC-4-8-TH (Special Theory of Relativity) [This course introduces special relativity, invariance and transformation rules, 4-vectors, homogeneous Lorentz group and Minkowski force].

      • PHS-A-CC-4-8-P [Mathematical Physics III (Practical)] [This course illustrates using Python on solution of ODEs,PDEs & BVPs, Fourier Series, Gaussian & Dirac-delta function, Complex Integral & Roots, Integral transform, Special functions].

  • Physics General (Semester System UG)

    1. Semester I: PHS-G-CC-1-1-TH (Oscillations & Elasticity) [This course discusses on the physics of Oscillations & theory of elasticity. This includes Simple Harmonic Motion, Superposition of SHM, forced damped SHM, Hooke's law, Torsion of Cylinder, Bending of beam, Twisting a wire etc].

    2. Semester II: PHS-G-CC-2-2-TH (Induction & Maxwell's Equation) [This course discusses on Faraday's law of EM induction & Maxwell's equations with EM wave propagation. This inludes Faraday/Lenz's discovery, Motional EMF, Self/Mutual inductance, Displacement Current, Poynting Vector, Polarization etc].

Previous Courses

  1. Environmental Sciences (Semester II) ENVC 24 (Energy and Environment) (PG) [The course introduces energy scales and their relative implications on the environment. After introducing the basics of conventional/non-conventional energy sources, the course unfolds into energy management/policies and energy security of Earth.] (2018)

  2. Physics Honours (UG)
    1. Paper VI (Unit II) (Solid State Physics I) [This course introduces Condensed Matter Physics. After briefing on Crystal/amorphous structure and X-ray diffraction, the course unfolds into crystal bonding, Free-electron theory and concludes with Band theory of solids.] (2017-19)

    2. Paper IIA (Unit I) (Thermal Physics I) [This course introduces Equilibrium & Non-equilibrium theory of Heat. Topics include assumptions of Kinetic Theory, distribution laws, Brownian Motion and transport coefficients, Non-ideal gases, Heat conduction and Blackbody radiation.] (2017)

    3. Paper IVA (Unit I) (Thermal Physics II) [This course introduces Thermodynamics, starting from 0th, 1st, 2nd and 3rd law, Maxwell relations and potentials, Phase Equilibria.] (2018-19)

    4. Paper VIIIB (Computer Laboratory) [This course introduces "Numerical Mathematics" in high-level language C. It includes basics of coding and performing arithmatic operations, solving algebraic equations in different geometry, root-finding, sorting and statistics calculation, matrix operations, playing with primes, iterative solvers, interpolation and integration methods.] (2017-19)

  3. Physics General (UG)
    1. Paper IB (Waves and Oscillations) [This course discusses on the physics of Waves and Vibrations, starting from SHM and superposition, damped and forced SHM, Wave equation in different geometry and Doppler effect.] (2017)

    2. Paper IVA (Communications) [This course discusses on principle of Communications. It motivates the subject by introducing propagation of EM wave, transmission of such waves through media, modulation and demodulation.] (2017-2018)

    3. Paper IVB (Computer Laboratory) [This courses teaches Numerical Mathematics in C. It includes basic coding and performing arithmatic operations, solving algebraic equations on different geometry, finding roots, sorting, series solution and matrix operations]. (2017-2019)
