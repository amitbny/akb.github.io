Topics

  • Simple Harmonic Motion: Differential equation and its solution.
  • Superposition of Simple Harmonic Motion: Analytical treatment, Lissajous figures, natural, damped and forced vibration, resonance, sharpness of resonance.
  • Differential equation of Wave Motion: Plane progressive wave - energy and intensity, Bel, decibel, and phon, Superposition of waves, beats, velocity of longitudinal waves in solid and gas, velocity of trasverse wave in string, Doppler effect.
